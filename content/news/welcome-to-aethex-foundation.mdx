After eight years of self-funded development, countless late nights, and a community that's grown to over 6,700 members — we're thrilled to announce the official launch of the **AeThex Foundation**.

## What is the Foundation?

The AeThex Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to building open-source digital infrastructure and empowering the next generation of creators through education and community programs.

We believe that the tools for building persistent digital worlds — identity, communication, state synchronization, and developer education — should be:

- **Open**: Transparent, inspectable, and free to use
- **Persistent**: Built to last, not to lock in
- **For everyone**: Accessible regardless of background or resources

## Why Now?

AeThex has always been about more than building games or platforms. From the beginning, our vision has been to create the foundational technology layer for digital civilization itself.

As our projects have matured — Passport, Connect, Engine Core — it became clear that the most important pieces should be protected. Not owned by a corporation that might change priorities. Not vulnerable to the whims of a single founder. But held in trust by a community-governed nonprofit. Forever.

## What Comes Next

The Foundation will steward:

1. **AeThex Passport** — Cross-platform identity and authentication
2. **AeThex Connect** — Real-time communication infrastructure
3. **AeThex Engine Core** — Our Godot-based game engine
4. **GameForge** — Our flagship educational program
5. **Community programs** — Documentation, mentorship, and more

We're also launching several new initiatives in 2026. Stay tuned for announcements about GameForge jams, contributor programs, and partnership opportunities.
