We're excited to announce **GameForge**, the AeThex Foundation's flagship educational initiative — a 48-hour game jam program designed to teach real-world development skills through hands-on building.

## What is GameForge?

GameForge isn't just another game jam. It's a structured learning experience where participants go from concept to playable prototype in a single weekend, guided by experienced mentors every step of the way.

Here's what makes GameForge different:

### Learn by Doing
Forget tutorials that collect dust. In GameForge, you ship something real. There's no better teacher than a deadline and a team counting on you.

### Mentorship Built In
Every jam includes access to experienced developers who've shipped games, built platforms, and learned from countless mistakes. They're here to help you avoid the same pitfalls.

### Community-First
You're not competing against others — you're building alongside them. GameForge emphasizes collaboration, peer learning, and community support.

### All Skill Levels Welcome
Whether you've never written a line of code or you're a seasoned developer looking for a challenge, GameForge has a track for you.

## The Format

Each GameForge event follows a proven structure:

| Phase | Duration | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Kickoff** | 2 hours | Theme reveal, team formation, brainstorming |
| **Build** | 40 hours | Development sprints with mentor check-ins |
| **Polish** | 4 hours | Bug fixes, playtesting, final touches |
| **Showcase** | 2 hours | Presentations, feedback, celebration |

## Why Game Jams?

Game development is one of the most comprehensive disciplines in software engineering. In 48 hours, you'll touch:

- Programming and architecture
- Art and visual design
- Audio and music
- Game design and UX
- Project management
- Team collaboration

These skills transfer to any tech career. And there's nothing quite like the feeling of shipping something playable in a weekend.
