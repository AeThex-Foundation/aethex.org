## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions of all sizes. Here's how to get started:

1. **Pick a project** that interests you
2. **Check the issues** labeled `good first issue` or `help wanted`
3. **Join our Discord** to connect with maintainers
4. **Submit a PR** — we review everything within 48 hours

Not sure where to start? Drop by `#dev-chat` on Discord and introduce yourself. We'll help you find a task that matches your skills and interests.

## What's Not on the Roadmap?

Some things we're explicitly *not* building:

- Blockchain/crypto integration (not aligned with our mission)
- Proprietary lock-in features
- Anything that compromises user privacy

If you see something on this roadmap that concerns you, or something missing that should be here — let us know. This is a community project, and your voice matters.
